Welcome to the Microsoft AI Decision Workshop

This workshop helps customers quickly distinguish between productivity copilots, low-code agents, custom AI app development, machine learning platforms, and analytics solutions. Each card presents a scenario, attendees answer from their phones, and the room discusses the recommended fit.
1
Rapid Fire: 30 seconds per scenario to keep energy high and test baseline product understanding.
2
Customer Workshop: use each answer reveal to ask why the scenario matters and what business outcome the customer wants.
3
Trap Mode: wrong options are intentionally plausible so the discussion sharpens positioning and differentiation.
